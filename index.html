About Us

Kornia AI is a non-profit organisation dedicated to advancing Spatial Artificial Intelligence through open collaboration. Originally founded in 2018 and officially registered as non-profit in 2023, Kornia AI is supported by an international community of contributors who drive its projects forward. The organization was initially built around Kornia, a widely adopted Python library for Differentiable Computer Vision, which has grown to over 2 million downloads per month and has been cited more than 400 times in Google Scholar. Now, we are expanding beyond open-source contributions to build AI tools to monetize that redefine how AI interacts with the physical world.

Open Source

We believe in the power of open source to drive innovation and collaboration. Our code is freely available for everyone to use, modify, and contribute to.

Community

Building and supporting a global community of computer vision enthusiasts and professionals. Join us in shaping the future of computer vision.

Innovation

Pushing the boundaries of what's possible in computer vision technology. We're constantly exploring new ideas and approaches.

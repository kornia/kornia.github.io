About kornia.ai

kornia.ai is a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing Spatial Artificial Intelligence through open collaboration. Founded in 2018 and officially registered as non-profit in 2023, we're supported by an international community of contributors driving innovation in computer vision.

Open Source First

We believe in the power of open source to drive innovation and collaboration. Our code is freely available for everyone to use, modify, and contribute to.

Global Community

Building and supporting a worldwide community of computer vision enthusiasts, researchers, and professionals shaping the future together.

Innovation at Scale

Pushing the boundaries of what's possible in computer vision technology while maintaining production-ready quality and performance.
